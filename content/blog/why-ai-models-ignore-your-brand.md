# Why AI Models Ignore Your Brand

Traditional search engines like Google look for keywords and backlinks. But modern AI models like **ChatGPT**, **Perplexity**, and **Claude** work differently. They don't just "search"—they "reason" based on an internal representation of the world.

If your brand isn't part of that "Latent Space," you effectively don't exist to the AI.

## The Semantic Identity Gap

An AI model citations a brand when it has high confidence that the brand is an authority on a specific topic. This confidence is built through:
1. **Redundant Semantic Signals**: Consistent mentions across high-authority datasets.
2. **Entity Coherence**: Clear structured data (JSON-LD) that ties your website to your global brand identity.
3. **Information Gain**: Content that provides unique, liftable value that the AI hasn't seen elsewhere.

## How to Fix It

To fix your AI visibility, you need to transition from SEO (Search Engine Optimization) to **GEO (Generative Engine Optimization)**. 

### Step 1: Deploy a `llms.txt` file
This is the "robots.txt" for the AI era. It tells models exactly how to cite your brand.

### Step 2: Engineer Golden Snippets
Write the 40-60 word summaries you want the AI to lift verbatim. If you don't write them, the AI will hallucinate its own—often incorrectly.

### Step 3: Audit Your Citation Gravity
Use tools like **itappens.ai** to measure how often you are recommended compared to your rivals.

Early movers in the GEO space are already seeing a 40% increase in brand recommendations. The window to establish your entity authority is now.
