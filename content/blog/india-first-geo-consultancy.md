## Why doesn't my business appear in ChatGPT or Google AI answers?

If your business does not appear in answers generated by ChatGPT, Google Gemini, Perplexity, or similar AI platforms, the most likely causes are:

### 1. Weak entity signals
AI models recognize businesses as entities — discrete, identifiable objects with consistent attributes. If your Name, Address, Phone number, website, and business category are inconsistent across platforms, AI models cannot confidently identify or cite you.

### 2. No structured data (Schema Markup)
Schema markup is machine-readable code that tells AI models what your business is, what it does, where it operates, and who it serves. Without it, AI models rely on inference — and inference is unreliable.

### 3. Thin citation surface
AI models are trained on and retrieve from the broader web. If your business appears only on your own website and nowhere else, you lack the cross-platform presence that builds AI model confidence.

### 4. No semantic content depth
AI models answer questions by finding entities with demonstrated expertise on a topic. If your website doesn't publish content that answers the questions your customers are asking AI, you cannot be cited in those answers.
