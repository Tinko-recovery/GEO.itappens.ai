# The 2026 GEO Window in India

India is currently at an inflection point. While traditional search still drives traffic, the **intent-to-discovery** path is shifting rapidly toward AI agents.

## The 73% Shift

Recent market data shows that **73% of modern users in India** prefer asking an AI assistant (like Perplexity or ChatGPT) for brand discovery rather than scrolling through pages of Google search results.

This isn't just a trend; it's a structural change in how commerce happens in the global South.

### Why 2026 is the "Seeding Year"

AI models go through training and fine-tuning cycles. If you start seeding your brand's semantic identity today, you are effectively "buying" your way into the training data of the next generation of models.

*   **First-Mover Advantage**: The first brands to be cited by AI for a category (e.g., "best boutique business hotel in Malur") become the "anchor entities" that models benchmark everyone else against.
*   **Trust compounding**: AI citation is a trust signal that compounds. The more a model cites you, the more confident its reasoning layer becomes in your authority.

## The Risk of Waiting

Brands that wait until 2027 to "start their SEO" will find themselves locked out of the AI citation loop. Traditional backlinks and keywords will not be enough to displace an entity that has already secured "Citation Gravity."

The window is closing. It's time to engineer your presence.
