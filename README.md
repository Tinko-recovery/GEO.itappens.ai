## 🚀 The Vision

## 📱 Real-Time Interaction (Telegram)
Stay in control without spending hours on meetings:
*   **30-Minute Reports**: Automatic status and spending updates every 30 minutes.
*   **Live Thought Feed**: See what agents are "thinking" as they work.
*   **Human-in-the-Loop**: Agents ping your Telegram when they need a decision. You reply via chat, and they keep moving.
